Potholes are a major source of a lot of problems we see on Indian roads. Too much traffic, slow moving traffic, blocked lanes, injuries and deaths, all of these issues are linked to potholes and bad roads in a major way. All roads lead to potholes.
To fill them we have to mark them first. What if citizens are given the responsibility and power to report and track potholes spread across their respective city?
